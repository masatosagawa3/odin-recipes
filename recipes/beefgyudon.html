Beef Gyudon

Description

Beef Gyudon is a Japanese rice bowl dish consisting of thinly sliced beef and onions simmered in a mildly sweet sauce flavored with soy sauce and mirin. It’s quick, satisfying, and delicious served over hot steamed rice.

Ingredients

  • 250g thinly sliced beef (ribeye or belly)
  • 1 medium onion, julliene cut
  • 3 tbsp shiro dashi (or water with instant dashi)
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sake
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • Cooked Japanese rice, for serving
  • Optional: pickled ginger and spring onions for garnish

Steps

  1. In a small pot, bring dashi, soy sauce, mirin, sake, and sugar to a simmer.
  2. Add sliced onions and cook until soft and translucent.
  3. Add beef slices and simmer until cooked through.
  4. Serve the beef and onions over a bowl of steamed rice with some sauce from the pot.
  5. Top with pickled ginger and spring onions if desired.
