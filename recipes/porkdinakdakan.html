Pork Dinakdakan

Description

Pork Dinakdakan is a popular Ilocano dish made from grilled or boiled pork parts mixed with onions, chili, and creamy mayonnaise or pig’s brain. It’s savory, tangy, and perfect as a pulutan or main dish with rice.

Ingredients

  • 500g pork belly (or face and ears, cleaned well)
  • 2 red onion, 1 brunoise 1 julliene
  • 2 green chillies (optional)
  • 2-3 red chilies, chopped
  • 3 tbsp calamansi juice
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise (or mashed pork brain, traditional style)
  • 2 tbsp sugar (optional)
  • Salt and pepper (you can use magic sarap for extra flavor and umami)
  • 3 Bayleaves

Steps

  1. Boil pork in salted water with bayleaves (you can also add onion and garlic for extra good smell) until tender, then grill or pan-sear until lightly charred.
  2. Slice pork into small pieces.
  3. In a bowl, combine pork, onion, and chili.
  4. Add calamansi juice and mix well. Let it sit for a few minutes to absorb flavor.
  5. Stir in mayonnaise (or mashed pork brain) and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve with hot rice or enjoy as pulutan!
