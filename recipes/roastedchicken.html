Roasted Chicken

Description

This simple Roasted Chicken recipe yields a juicy, golden-brown chicken with crispy skin and tender meat. It’s seasoned with basic herbs and spices — perfect for a comforting meal with family or friends.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (about 1-1.5 kg)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil or melted butter
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp black peppercorn
  • 1 tbsp corriander seeds
  • 1 tsp cinnamon powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Fresh herbs (rosemary, thyme, or parsley)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (400°F).
  2. Pat the chicken dry with paper towels.
  3. roast salt, black peppercorn, corriander seeds, cinnamon powder, and paprika in a frying pan all together until it releases aroma
  4. Grind all the roasted spices (you can use manual spice grinder)
  5. Rub the chicken all over with olive oil or butter, then season it with all of the spices.
  6. Stuff the cavity with lemon halves and herbs.
  7. Place the chicken on a roasting pan and bake for 1 hour to 1 hour 15 minutes, or until the juices run clear.
  8. Let rest for 10 minutes before carving. Serve warm!
